How it Works

The Equation:
2H2O + heat ⇌ H3O+ + OH−
Endothermic: Heat is a reactant. Increasing Temp shifts equilibrium right.
Le Châtelier: Adding ions creates "stress." The system reacts to restore equilibrium.
